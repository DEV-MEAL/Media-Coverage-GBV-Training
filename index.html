Media coverage and reporting
related to GBV issue

Recognizing the pivotal role that media plays in conveying messages and promoting social causes and concerns. Amel Association and Plan international are organizing two days’ workshop on “Media Coverage and Reporting on GBV Issues”. The training is targeting 25 participants from youth-led organizations and involving journalist from various media to support in communication and advocacy for GBV issues. At the end of the workshop, the participants will be able to report on topics of their concern related to gender-equality and advocacy using gender-sensitive and girls’ right language, identify and analyze GBV related issues and to advocate for a change through social media platforms and other media channels.
